Philosophy Punks

[say hello to the punk-philosophers]

David Hume

While not the most punk-looking of philosophers, we all know and love Hume for his extraordinary number of references to playing billiards and drinking wine. This punk held nothing back, gunning down one philosopher after another.

John Heil

Similarly to Hume, Heil does not hesitate to destroy his colleagues. Part of his punky charm is his tendency to dismiss every view but his own as "stupid".

Andy Clark

Well, just look at him - there's no way this philosopher could fall under our punk radar. Clark is the cool philosopher of mind that we all want to watch bad 80s movies with.

Carl Craver

There are rumors that this "Master of Imagination Bombs" headed a philosphy-themed punk band in the St. Louis area. Whiny songs about being a brain in a vat? Yes, please, Dr. Craver.
